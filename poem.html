The Journey

A Poem for the Digital Age

In lines of code and words we weave,
A world that's built on what we believe,
Each keystroke marks a step we take,
With every choice we make and remake.
Through mountains high and valleys deep,
We climb the goals we wish to keep,
The path is long, the light is bright,
We journey through the endless night.
So take a breath and look around,
In every soul, new strength is found,
For life's a poem, lines so true,
And every verse begins with you.
